Welcome to Amblyfy Eye Test

We are glad You decided to check your vision. It's the first step to fixing if anything is not as you like.

This eye test will help you:

  • • Check your visual acuity
  • • Test for astigmatism
  • • Measure contrast sensitivity
  • • Get personalized recommendations

The test takes about 5-10 minutes to complete. Make sure you're in a well-lit room and have your reading glasses ready if you use them.
